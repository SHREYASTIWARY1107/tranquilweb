Our Story

Learn about our mission to make mental health support accessible to everyone through technology.

Our Mission

At Tranquil AI, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality mental health support. Our mission is to leverage artificial intelligence to make therapy, mindfulness, and emotional wellness tools accessible to all, regardless of location, schedule, or financial constraints.

We're committed to breaking down barriers to mental healthcare and creating a world where seeking help for mental health is as normal and accessible as physical health.

Our Journey

From idea to mental health innovation

2022

The Beginning

Tranquil AI was founded by a team of mental health professionals, AI researchers, and individuals who experienced firsthand the challenges of accessing mental health support.

2023

Initial Research

Our team conducted extensive research on AI applications in mental health, working with therapists and psychologists to develop evidence-based approaches.

2024

Beta Launch

We launched our beta version to select users, gathering valuable feedback and continuously improving our AI algorithms and user experience.

2025

Public Release

Tranquil AI is now available to everyone, with ongoing updates and new features based on user feedback and the latest research in mental health.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Compassion

We approach mental health with empathy and understanding, recognizing that each person's journey is unique.

Privacy

We prioritize the security and confidentiality of our users' data, creating a safe space for mental health support.

Accessibility

We're committed to making mental health support available to everyone, regardless of background or circumstances.

Evidence-Based

Our approach is grounded in psychological research and proven therapeutic techniques.

Supporting UN Sustainable Development Goals

Tranquil AI is proud to contribute to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, particularly Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being. By making mental health support more accessible, we're working toward a healthier, more resilient global community.
