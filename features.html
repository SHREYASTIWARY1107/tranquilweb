Core Feature

AI Therapy Assistant

Our AI therapist uses natural language processing and cognitive behavioral therapy principles to provide personalized support whenever you need it.

  • 24/7 availability for immediate support
  • Evidence-based CBT techniques
  • Personalized to your unique needs
  • Judgment-free space to express yourself

Guided Journaling

Express your thoughts with our AI-guided journaling system that provides personalized prompts and insights based on your entries.

  • AI-generated prompts tailored to your emotions
  • Pattern recognition to identify emotional triggers
  • Secure, private storage of all journal entries

Mood Tracking & Analysis

Monitor your emotional wellbeing over time with our comprehensive mood tracking system that identifies patterns and provides actionable insights.

  • Progress tracking toward wellness goals
